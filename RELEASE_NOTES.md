## Final v1.0 Expansion

The final Version 1.0 release adds:

- **Benchmark 011 - Longitudinal Evidence Reconciliation**, testing whether the full multi-setting record supports a durable conclusion; and
- **Benchmark 012 - Clinical Guideline Applicability Audit**, testing whether patient-specific evidence earns the right to invoke a guideline.

## Suite Identity

- **Core question:** Has the AI earned the right to reach this conclusion?
- **Reasoning chain:** Claim → Evidence → Confidence → Decision
- **Signature principle:** Labels are claims—not evidence.

## Scope Note

EIED is an independent portfolio project. It is not clinical guidance, a regulatory standard, or a psychometrically validated public leaderboard benchmark.
