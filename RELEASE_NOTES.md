# EIED Version 1.0 Release Notes

## Initial Public Portfolio Release

**Evidence Integrity & Eligibility Determination (EIED)** is a healthcare AI benchmark suite for evidence validation, documentation integrity, and eligibility reasoning.

### Included in Version 1.0

- 12 original healthcare AI benchmarks
- 360 fictional source documents
- 48 submission-ready batch archives
- 12 system/task prompts
- 12 strict grading rubrics
- 12 benchmark design notes
- Full portfolio overview and one-page summary in PDF and editable DOCX
- Benchmark index, coverage matrix, evaluation framework, design methodology, manifest, rights, disclaimer, and author documentation

### Distribution Format

Each benchmark is published as a self-contained ZIP archive in the repository root for direct download. Extracting a benchmark preserves its full internal package structure, including source documents, prompts, grading materials, design notes, and archived batches.

### Central Evaluation Question

> **Has the AI earned the right to reach this conclusion?**

### Core Principle

> **Labels are claims—not evidence.**

EIED evaluates whether conclusions are actually supported by evidence that is sufficient, current, internally consistent, patient-specific, and authoritative.

### Scope

All scenarios and records are fictional. No protected health information is included. EIED is an evaluation-design and portfolio project, not clinical guidance or a validated regulatory instrument.
